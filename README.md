Welcome to my collection of 3D models designed for 3D printing.
This repository serves as a personal workspace where I share projects I’ve created, experimented with, or am still developing.

About the Models
- These models are work-in-progress and may not be perfect.
- Some designs are unfinished or experimental, but I believe sharing them can still be useful.
- Feel free to explore, adapt, or improve them for your own projects.

Purpose

The goal of this repository is to:
- Provide a place to store and organize different designs.
- Share ideas that others might find inspiring or helpful.

How to Use

If you’d like to print or modify these models, here’s the setup I typically use:
- 3D Printer: Bambu Lab P1S
- Slicer Software: Bambu Studio (for preparing models to print)
- Modeling Software: FreeCAD (for creating and editing designs)
- Filament: PLA (99% of the time)
- Profiles: either BambuLab PLA Basic or Generic PLA (for all other PLA filaments)

⚠️ Note: Settings may vary depending on your printer, slicer, or filament brand. Always check and adjust parameters such as layer height, infill, and supports to suit your hardware and preferences.
